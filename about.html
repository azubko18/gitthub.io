    Nice to meet you!

    Hello! I became interested in swimming at the age of 9 and decided to go to a sports school. Combining study and 2 workouts per day is quite difficult. But children have enough energy for both. Also, children learn time management, become more disciplined, purposeful and healthy. By the time I finished school, I was a two-time champion of Belarus in swimming among juniors (2013, 2014), as well as a champion of Europe (2014). I am a Master of Sports of Belarus.
    I was an assistant coach for Olympic athletes. Now I am a personal swimming coach. I offer group workouts for children as well as individual swimming lessons for adults and children.
